Design Process Document

1. The Problem: How much sleep do you need? If I sleep too much is that as bad for my mental health/mood as sleeping too little? How much sleep is right for me?

2. Requirements: The project should track sleep and mood, compare them, and attempt to find an optimal amount of sleep for the user.
